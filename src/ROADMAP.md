# AI Platform Explorer - Strategic Roadmap

**Version:** 3.0 | **Status:** Active | **Target:** Enterprise AI Selection Teams

This document outlines the strategic evolution of the AI Platform Explorer through Phases 3 and 4, enabling "Decision Intelligence" and "Operational Ecosystems".

---

## Phase 3: Decision Intelligence & Insights
**Focus:** Leveraging data and algorithms to automate recommendations, monitor health, and validate value.

### 3.1 "Best Fit" Recommendation Engine
**Objective:** Automate matching of organizational requirements to platform capabilities.
*   **Features:** Requirements Wizard, Vector Matching Algorithm, Gap Analysis.

### 3.2 Real-time Vendor Risk Monitor
**Objective:** Track operational stability and reputational risk.
*   **Features:** API Status Feeds, Security Incident Aggregation, Risk Score Trending.

### 3.3 TCO & ROI Validation Tracker
**Objective:** Move from projected ROI to actual value realization.
*   **Features:** Implementation Phase Tracking, Actual vs. Projected Variance, Adoption Rate Inputs.

### 3.4 Collaborative Workspace
**Objective:** Transform analysis into a living team document.
*   **Features:** Inline Comments, Approval Workflows, @Mentions.

### 3.5 Automated Reporting Studio
**Objective:** Generate executive-ready artifacts.
*   **Features:** PDF Export, Executive Summary Generator, Compliance Slide Decks.

---

## Phase 4: Ecosystem & Operational Automation
**Focus:** Integrating the selection platform into the broader enterprise tech stack and procurement workflow.

### 4.1 Enterprise Integration Hub
**Objective:** Connect with downstream tools.
*   **Features:** Jira/Linear Sync, Slack Notifications, Procurement ERP Export.

### 4.2 Custom Extension Marketplace
**Objective:** Allow organizations to add proprietary platforms or plugins.
*   **Features:** Custom Model Import, Internal Tool Benchmarking, Plugin Architecture.

### 4.3 AI-Assisted RFP Generator
**Objective:** Automate the procurement documentation process.
*   **Features:** Spec-to-RFP Conversion, Vendor Question Generation, Scoring Sheet Export.

### 4.4 Continuous Compliance Agent
**Objective:** Automatically monitor platform terms and certifications.
*   **Features:** TOS Change Detection, Cert Expiry Alerts, Sub-processor Updates.

### 4.5 Global Governance Console
**Objective:** Manage multi-org usage for large enterprises.
*   **Features:** Cross-BU Benchmarking, Global Policy Enforcement, Usage Audit Logs.
