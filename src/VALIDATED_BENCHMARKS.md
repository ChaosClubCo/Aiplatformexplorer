## üéì Usage Guidelines

### For Presentations
1. **Lead with validated metrics:** Start with Capgemini 1.7x average
2. **Cite sources:** Always include "(Source Name, Year)"
3. **Set expectations:** Use conservative estimates, not optimistic
4. **Show range:** Present conservative/midpoint/optimistic scenarios
5. **Address risks:** Proactively mention Gartner 30% abandonment

### For Calculations
1. **Default to midpoint:** Use $13,350/employee/year productivity
2. **Apply industry multiplier:** Adjust for your sector
3. **Include change management:** 1:1 budget ratio
4. **Factor adoption rate:** Use realistic percentage (50-70% without exec sponsor)
5. **Add support costs:** 15% of platform cost annually

### For Risk Assessment
1. **Data quality first:** Assess before committing
2. **Executive sponsorship:** Secure before pilot
3. **Clear objectives:** Define measurable outcomes
4. **Governance:** Establish AI committee
5. **Kill criteria:** Define go/no-go thresholds
